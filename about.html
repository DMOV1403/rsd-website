Our story

Where it began

RSD started with a simple conviction: that Oman's natural wealth is worth far more when it is developed with foresight and connected to the world with intent. Rather than treat resources, capital and industry as separate trades, we built one company to carry all three.

The name holds that idea. RSD comes from Rasad, the old Arabic word for watching the sky and reading what is coming. It is the word Arab astronomers used. It still describes how we work: observe closely, then act with precision.

Vision & Mission

What drives us

Our vision

To be the trusted bridge between Oman's natural resources and the international partners who build the future with them.

Our mission

To develop resources, structure investment and apply technology with foresight and care, for our partners, our country and the long term.

What we value

Four things we hold to

Foresight

We watch what others miss. Every decision starts with reading the ground and the horizon before we move.

Precision

Accurate and grounded. We name things plainly, with no guesswork and no overstatement.

Partnership

We are mindful of the partners, regulators and regions we work with, and we never treat a relationship as a transaction.

Endurance

We build for the long term. The work should still stand, and still matter, years from now.
