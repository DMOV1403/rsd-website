Founding story

Where it began

RSD was founded on a straightforward conviction: that resources, capital and technology are strongest when they work together rather than apart. Where others treat mining, investment and industrial technology as separate worlds, we built a company that connects them.

From the outset, the intent was to operate with calm authority — to identify genuine opportunity, structure it responsibly, and build operations that endure beyond any single project.

Vision & Mission

Our vision

To be a trusted multi-sector company recognised for building enduring value across mining, investment and industrial technology.

Our mission

To identify resources, structure investment and apply technology with discipline and respect — for our partners, the regions we work in, and the long term.
