What We Do

Three sectors.
One company.

Mining, investment and industrial technology. Different work, joined by a single way of operating and a single goal: turning Omani resources into lasting value.

01

Mining

RSD develops Oman's natural resources, with gypsum as our lead. We take minerals from assessment and extraction through to processing and export, supplying industrial buyers in India, the Philippines and other growing markets.

Gypsum leads the way, with salt and precipitated calcium carbonate close behind. Everything is built around responsible development and supply our partners can count on.

Scope & Approach
  • Resource development, led by gypsum
  • Salt and precipitated calcium carbonate
  • Mineral processing and industrial cities
  • Export-focused supply to India, the Philippines and beyond
02

Investment

RSD is the bridge between Omani opportunity and international capital. We structure the partnerships and investment that bring European and global investors into large-scale development in Oman.

We look for genuine, long-term opportunity and back it with careful structuring, aligned interests and clear terms.

Scope & Approach
  • Connecting international investors with Omani opportunity
  • Deal structuring and capital allocation
  • Large-scale development partnerships, anchored in Duqm
  • Long-term value, built alongside our partners
03

Industrial Technology

RSD builds the industrial backbone that resources and investment depend on, from ports and logistics to the data infrastructure of the years ahead.

In Duqm that means container terminal and logistics capacity, mineral processing cities, and AI-ready data centers designed for geographic redundancy and powered by green hydrogen.

Scope & Approach
  • Port and logistics infrastructure, including container terminals
  • Mineral processing and industrial cities
  • AI and data center infrastructure with geographic redundancy
  • Powered by green hydrogen, built for the long term
