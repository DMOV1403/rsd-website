# RSD Website Draft

A static, brand-aligned draft for **RSD**, an Omani venture company.
Tagline: *Where Vision Builds.*

The name comes from Rasad (رصد), the Arabic word for observation and foresight.
That idea (look closely, then build) runs through the copy without being spelled out.

## Pages
| File | Page | Covers |
|------|------|--------|
| `index.html` | Home | Hero, intro, three sector cards, featured Duqm gypsum project, contact CTA |
| `about.html` | About | Story and name origin, vision and mission, values, founders, regions |
| `what-we-do.html` | What We Do | Mining, Investment, Industrial Technology, with scope each |
| `projects.html` | Projects | Filterable grid: gypsum, salt/PCC, container terminal, AI/data, investment |
| `contact.html` | Contact | Form (name, company, email, message) plus email, phone, Duqm base, LinkedIn |

## What RSD does
A bridge between Omani natural resources and international buyers and investors,
with large scale development focused in Duqm, Oman.
1. **Mining** (Omani resources, gypsum lead; also salt and PCC; export to India and the Philippines)
2. **Investment** (connecting European and global capital with Omani opportunity)
3. **Industrial Technology** (ports and logistics, mineral processing cities, green hydrogen powered AI/data infrastructure)

## Brand
- **Palette** Obsidian `#0A0A0A` and deep blues carry the brand; **red** `#C8102E` is the primary accent; **teal** `#16A39A` marks partnership and specialty. Ivory `#FEF9F2` and white for content areas.
- **Type** Montserrat throughout.
- **Logo** Supplied manually (three crescents for vision, strategy, execution, wordmark inside the curves). Used as-is; no placeholder mark is generated.
- **Voice** Concise, confident, measured. One human voice. No em dashes anywhere.
